About Us

When COVID-19 quarantine hit, we had a lot of spare time on our hands. And, like a lot of people, we used that time to read, play, and binge watch. There are a lot of review websites out there, but we wanted to combine them and use a simple review format!

Our format is separated into Rose, Bud, and Thorn. For the sake of everyone's good fun, try to remain spoiler free, or at least announce any spoilers before they can be read. The first section, Rose, is where you write all of the things that you love about the thing you're reviewing! Did the game have a great storyline? Did that movie have you on the edge of your seat? Write it all here!

In the Bud section, write something that you wish would be improved, or something that you hope they will add. Did that video game have an interesting side story that you want a DLC for? Or maybe that TV show had an interesting character that you hope gets a bigger role. This is the section for those thoughts.

As for the Thorn section, this is where you will write any problems or dislikes that you had. Was that tabletop game missing pieces? Maybe that book series never tied of all those loose ends, or the author killed off your favorite character. Any complaints go here!
